### 2. Landscaping/Lawn Care Pain Points to Emphasize

- Seasonal demand spikes (spring openings, leaf clean‑ups, projects).  
- Owners/foremen in the field, not answering every call.  
- Missed opportunities for **recurring contracts** and add‑on work.  
- Slow follow‑up on design/build and enhancement quotes.

Example line:

> “Most homeowners and property managers go with whoever gets them a proposal and start date fastest. If calls slip or quotes sit, the work goes elsewhere.”
