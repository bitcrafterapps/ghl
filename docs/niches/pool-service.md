### 1. Pool Service‑Specific Hero

**Headline:**  
Keep Your Pool Routes Full With More Booked Cleanings & Repairs

**Subheadline:**  
We install an AI‑powered booking & follow‑up system for pool service companies that captures missed calls, books cleanings and repairs, and keeps follow‑ups and renewals on autopilot — without adding office staff.

**Credibility Line:**  
Built for residential and small commercial pool companies offering cleaning, maintenance, and repairs.

---

### 2. Pool Service Pain Points to Emphasize

- Seasonal demand spikes at the start of the season and before holidays.  
- Homeowners calling multiple companies trying to get on a **weekly route or one‑time cleaning** fast.  
- Office/owner juggling phones while in the field, leading to **missed calls and voicemails**.  
- Weak follow‑up on **equipment repairs, upgrades, and renewals** of service plans.

Example line:

> “Most pool owners just want someone reliable who can get them on the schedule quickly. If calls go to voicemail or quotes sit in your inbox, those routes go to another company.”

---

### 3. Pool Service Use Cases for the Solution Section

Tune your “What’s Included” like this:

- Missed call → SMS:  
  “Sorry we missed your call — are you looking for pool cleaning, ongoing service, or a repair?”

- AI intake questions:
  - Service type (one‑time clean, weekly service, opening/closing, repair).  
  - Pool type and size (in‑ground/above‑ground, approximate size).  
  - Issues (green water, cloudy, equipment problem, leak, other).  
  - Location and timing (ASAP, this week, schedule for later).

- Automatic booking:
  - Book **service visits, openings/closings, and repair appointments** into your calendar.

- Follow‑up:
  - Follow‑up on **repair quotes and equipment upgrades** (pumps, heaters, automation).  
  - Renewal reminders for **weekly/bi‑weekly service plans**.  
  - Review and referral requests after successful jobs.

### 4. Pool Service Results & Example Math

Typical ranges:

- One‑time service: \$100–\$300  
- Weekly service plans: \$120–\$300/month per client  
- Equipment repairs/upgrades: \$500–\$5,000+  

Example scenario:

> “If we help you add just **5–10 more weekly service clients** and recover a few extra repair/upgrade jobs each month, the system more than pays for itself from customers you’re already attracting.”
