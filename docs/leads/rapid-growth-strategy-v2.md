## Core Philosophy: The Kai Stone Approach

### What Works (Proven 10x Better Performance)
1. **Raw, authentic video content** - Whiteboard videos with zero edits outperform polished ads by 10x
2. **Low-barrier entry pricing** - $297/month disrupts an industry charging $2,000-$5,000
3. **Prove value first** - Let results speak, then upsell
4. **Speak like a human** - Contractors hate feeling sold to; be real
5. **Eat your own dog food** - Use your AI system to respond to YOUR leads instantly

### What to Avoid
- Polished, "Hollywood-style" ads
- Cold calling (businesses review agency partnerships only 1-2x per year)
- Generic, template-based outreach
- High-pressure sales tactics
- Slow response times (ironic if you're selling speed-to-lead)

## PHASE 1: 10 Clients in 7 Days

### Day 1: Foundation (8 hours)

**Morning (4 hours): Setup Your Own System**

Before selling, make sure YOUR lead response is instant:
1. Set up GHL sub-account for your agency
2. Configure AI speed-to-lead (respond in <60 seconds)
3. Create booking calendar with 15-minute demo slots
4. Set up follow-up automation (text + email)

**Afternoon (4 hours): Create Your Video Arsenal**

Record these 5 raw, whiteboard-style videos (no editing needed):

1. **The Problem Video** (2-3 min)
   - "Here's what happens when a contractor misses a call at 7pm..."
   - Draw simple diagram: Missed Call → Lost Job → $2,000 gone
   - Hook: "Contractors, you're losing $6,000/month and don't know it"

2. **The Demo Video** (3-4 min)
   - Live walkthrough: Call comes in → AI responds → Books appointment
   - Show actual text messages and calendar booking
   - "This is running 24/7 for you"

3. **The Math Video** (2 min)
   - "If you miss just 3 calls per month worth $2,000 each..."
   - Show ROI calculation: $6,000 lost vs $297 investment
   - Whiteboard the numbers

4. **The Comparison Video** (2 min)
   - "Here's what agencies charge vs. what I charge..."
   - Whiteboard: $2,000-5,000/mo vs $297/mo
   - "I'm willing to prove it first"

5. **The Proof Video** (2-3 min)
   - Screenshots of actual recovered leads
   - "This HVAC company booked 14 extra jobs in 30 days"
   - If no clients yet, use your own demo as proof

### Day 2: Platform Setup & Warm Network (8 hours)

### Day 7: Close & Convert

**Demo Call Framework (15 minutes)**

**1. Rapport & Discovery (4 min)**
- "Thanks for taking the time. Before I show you anything, tell me about your business."
- "How do leads typically come in for you?"
- "Who handles calls after 5pm and on weekends?"
- "What's an average job worth for you?"
- "How many calls do you think you might be missing?"

**2. Problem Framing (2 min)**
- "So if you're missing even 2-3 calls per month at $[their average]..."
- "That's $[X] gone. And worse, those customers just called your competitor."
- "The first company to respond wins 78% of the time."

**3. Demo (5 min)**
- Call the demo number live
- Show: Missed call → AI text in 30 seconds → Customer replies → Booking
- "This is running 24/7 for you. Christmas morning, 2am, whenever."
- Show the dashboard: "Here's where you see every conversation"

**4. Handle Objections (2 min)**

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I need to think about it" | "Totally get it. What specifically do you want to think through? Usually it's either price, timing, or 'does this actually work.' Which is it for you?" |
| "It's too expensive" | "I hear you. Let me ask - what's one job worth to you? [$X]. So if this recovers even ONE job you would have missed, it pays for itself for [Y] months. And we're offering it at $297 - most companies charge $2,000+." |
| "I don't trust AI" | "Smart to be skeptical. Let me show you exactly what it sounds like. [Show sample conversations]. These aren't robots - they're trained specifically for your industry. And you can customize everything." |
| "I already have a receptionist" | "That's great for business hours. What about 6pm when they go home? Weekends? The average contractor misses 76% of after-hours calls. This handles those." |
| "Let me talk to my partner" | "Makes sense. What questions will they have? Let me give you everything you need. Actually, are they available for a quick 5-minute call right now?" |

**5. Close (2 min)**
- "Based on what you've seen, does this make sense for [Company Name]?"
- "I'm doing $0 setup for my first 10 clients this week - you'd be number [X]."
- "All I need is your business name and we can have this live within 24 hours."
- Take payment: Stripe or direct invoice

**Day 7 Targets**
- 15-25 demos booked (from video + referrals + ads)
- 10 clients closed at 40-50% close rate
- If lower, extend to Day 8-10
