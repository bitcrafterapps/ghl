## Objection Handling Guide {#objection-handling}

### Price Objections

**"It's too expensive" / "$297 is a lot"**
```
Response: "I hear you. Let me ask - what's one job worth to you?

[Wait for answer: $X]

So if this recovers even ONE job you would have missed, it pays for itself
for [calculate months].

And we're offering it at $297 - most companies charge $2,000 to $5,000
for less. I just want to prove it works first."
```

**"I need to think about it"**
```
Response: "Totally understand. What specifically do you want to think through?

Usually it's one of three things - price, timing, or 'does this actually work.'
Which one is it for you?

[Address the specific concern]

The reason I ask is - every day you wait is another day of missed calls.
I just calculated you might be missing $[X] per month. Want to start
capturing those now or...?"
```

**"Can you do a discount?"**
```
Response: "Here's the thing - at $297, we're already a fraction of what
others charge. I've seen agencies charge $5,000 for less than this.

But here's what I CAN do - let me waive the setup fee for you. That's
normally $297, so you're essentially getting your first month free.

Fair?"
```

### Trust Objections

**"I don't trust AI"**
```
Response: "Smart to be skeptical. Most people picture some robotic voice
saying 'PLEASE HOLD' [laugh].

Let me show you exactly what it sounds like.

[Show sample conversations]

See how natural that is? The customer doesn't even know it's AI. They just
know they got a response in 30 seconds instead of waiting for a callback.

And you can customize everything - the tone, the questions it asks, when
to escalate to you directly."
```

**"What if it says something wrong?"**
```
Response: "Great question. The AI is trained specifically on YOUR business.
Your services, your pricing, your service area.

And for anything it's unsure about, it won't make stuff up - it'll say
'Let me have our team follow up with you directly' and escalate to you.

Plus you can see every conversation. If something's off, we adjust it.
Takes 5 minutes."
```

### Timing Objections

**"Let me talk to my partner/wife"**
```
Response: "Makes total sense. What questions will they have?

Actually - are they available right now? I can do a quick 5-minute
overview for both of you. Easier than you trying to explain it.

[If no:] No worries. When are you two usually together? I can send you a
summary doc and schedule a quick follow-up call."
```

**"We're in our slow season"**
```
Response: "Actually, that's the BEST time to set this up.

When busy season hits, you'll be slammed. You won't have time to
implement anything. But if you set this up now, it's ready when
calls start flooding in.

Plus, even in slow season - can you really afford to miss calls?
Every lead counts more when they're sparse."
```

**"I need to see it work first"**
```
Response: "That's literally what the first month is for.

This isn't a contract. If you don't see results in 30 days, cancel.
No hard feelings.

But if you're like our other clients, you'll see your first recovered
lead within the first week. Then it's a no-brainer.

What do you have to lose? Less than $300 to test it?"
```

### Competition Objections

**"I already have a receptionist"**
```
Response: "That's awesome - you're ahead of a lot of contractors.

But let me ask: What happens at 6pm when they go home? Saturdays?
Sundays? Holidays?

This isn't meant to replace your receptionist. It's for the 128 hours
a week she's NOT there. The average contractor misses 76% of their
after-hours calls.

Think of it as your night shift and weekend coverage. For less than
hiring someone part-time."
```

**"I tried something like this before and it didn't work"**
```
Response: "What was it, if you don't mind me asking?

[Listen to their experience]

Okay, so [problem they had]. Here's how this is different...

[Address their specific concern - usually it's either the AI wasn't
trained well OR the follow-up wasn't automated OR it was too
expensive]

Tell you what - let's do a pilot. Give me 30 days. If you don't see
results that blow away what you tried before, I'll give you a full
refund. No questions."
```

### Stall Tactics

**"Send me some information"**
```
Response: "I can definitely do that. But honestly, the info won't be
as helpful as actually SEEING it work.

It's a 15-minute demo. Can we find 15 minutes later this week?
I promise it's worth it.

[If they resist:] Okay, what's your email? I'll send you a case study
and a recording of the demo. Fair warning - the recording doesn't do
it justice. Let me know if you want to see it live after."
```

**"I'm too busy right now"**
```
Response: "I get it - you're running a business. Totally understand.

But think about it this way: you're too busy to take 15 minutes,
but you're not too busy to miss thousands of dollars in calls every month?

This is literally going to SAVE you time. Less chasing leads, more
automatically booked appointments.

What if we did 7am before your day starts? Or 6pm after things
calm down?"
```
